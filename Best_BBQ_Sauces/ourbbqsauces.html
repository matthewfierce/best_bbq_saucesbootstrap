Our Flavors

Sweet Tangy BBQ

The original recipe of our Grandfather. This sweet Texas style BBQ made from all natural ingredients will knock off any of the competitions BBQ due to its sweet flavor and rich texturing. This recipe has never been touched and will be the old classic of Dads BBQ.

Habanero Devil Fire

We are sure to knock you back a few with our special spicy BBQ sauce that will have you sweating by the time can is emptied. This sauce is mixed with 10 different spices and pepers that makes this our patented spicy BBQ. Voted 2nd most hottest sauce since 2014 in all of Texas and we plan to keep making our recipe even hotter with time.

Alabama Zesty White BBQ

Our Alabama Zesty White BBQ sauce was inspired in 1974 in Albama by our grandfather. This sauce is a cool and tangy BBQ that is extremely unique, and renowned throughout resturauants all over America. This sauce is something for people who love a sweet tangy flavor and cool ranch feeling. This one is perfect for any brisket or choice of meat you are wanting to use.

North Carolina Reaper

Our North Carolina Reaper is our unique blend of ghost peppers mixed with other herbs and spices. This sauce is mixed with specific herbs and spices that are native to North Carolina to give it that authetic North Carolina feeling. This one will give you a perfect blend between sweet and spicy.The perfect blend for people who want just the right amount of kick to it, but still want that sweet Dad's BBQ taste.
